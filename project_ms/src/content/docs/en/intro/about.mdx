---
title: About Project MS
---



**Project MS** (Project Management System) is a web app made for IT students and teachers at OAMK

It helps manage projects done together with companies.

Before, Excel sheets were used to track projects. Now, everything is in one place.

**What this tool does:**

* Makes it easy to organize student projects
* Improves communication between students and teachers
* Keeps everything clear and simple
